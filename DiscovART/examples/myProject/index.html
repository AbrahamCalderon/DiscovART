My name is Flournoyd DuVal Brown, 44years of age, father of 3, Chicago native, and a self-taught interdisciplinary artist. I'm an art enthusiast. Painting is my first love, be it on canvas, automobiles, boats, skin, and even tattoos. I have an associate degree in Sociology, it comes in handy when interacting with people, understanding the why of human behavior and rendering a conversation piece.
